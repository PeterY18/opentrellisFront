Apply to Startup Works

Joining a group of other founders for 17 weeks of learning and growing a business is a big commitment and you've taken the first step. Once you complete this online application it will be reviewed, and the program facilitators will reach out to schedule an interview.

Quick note on cost -- While the program does have a fee, the cost is adjusted based on personal income, which will be discussed during the interview. Financial assistance is available, and to qualify you must bring the prior year's tax return to the interview as proof of previous income.
