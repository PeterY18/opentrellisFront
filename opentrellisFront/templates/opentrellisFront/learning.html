Learning

Business founders are continuously gathering new information, testing assumptions, making adjustments and measuring results. It can be difficult to get straightforward answers to our questions. So, we’ve developed ways to collaborate with one another, to help cultivate each other’s businesses, and to learn from each other.

Richmond, Virginia

RVA Works has helped over 250 entrepreneurs present their businesses, and has built a community of over 6,000 founders and those supporting them. By assisting in the organization of our Richmond community of 1 Million Cups, we provide an inclusive learning environment for founders to learn from each other, to present their business (no pitches), to generate feedback, and for audience members to build local connections.

Through our core acceleration program, we have assisted business owners starting or formalizing their business:

  • 124 Founders have entered the core training program
  • Two-thirds of graduates start their business
  • 38% - The average annual income increase for graduates who launched their business*
    • *Past performance is not a guarantee of future results
